ABOUT PAK NON

Preserving Malaysia's Wau Heritage

Mr. Samat bin Man, better known as Pak Non, is a renowned traditional wau (kite) craftsman from Perlis who has been actively producing wau since 1972. Born on 18 May 1946 in Kampung Tok Kaya, Kuala Perlis, he learned the art of wau making from a Kelantan master during the 1970s. Over the years, he has become one of Malaysia's leading wau makers, producing various traditional designs such as Wau Helang, Wau Bulan, Wau Kucing, Wau Puyuh, Wau Merak, and Wau Jala Budi. His handcrafted wau are widely used in competitions, displayed as souvenirs, and commissioned by prestigious organisations, including Malaysia Airlines (MAS) and the Istana Arau. Pak Non is also dedicated to preserving the craft by conducting workshops, teaching younger generations, and passing his skills to two of his children. His commitment to quality craftsmanship and cultural preservation has earned him numerous awards, including the Tukang Ulung Kraf Award (2014), Pingat Jasa Kebaktian (PJK) (2014), and the title of Adiguru Kraf Wau (2018).
